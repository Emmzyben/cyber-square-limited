Our Services

Consulting and Advisory Services

Cyber Square Limited’s Consulting and Advisory Services offer comprehensive support across various areas, including risk assessment, security strategy development, security architecture design, compliance management, incident response planning, and more.

Our experienced advisory experts provide tailored guidance and oversight, ensuring that your security strategy is not only aligned with your business objectives but also reflects project priorities and industry best practices. We recommend effective security policies, monitor the strength of your information security program, and ensure your organization remains compliant with applicable laws, regulations, and contractual obligations.

Service Offering

• IT Governance

We help establish robust IT governance frameworks, ensuring that your IT operations align with your business goals and comply with industry standards.

• PCI-DSS Advisory

We provide advisory services to ensure your organization is compliant with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI-DSS), protecting sensitive cardholder data.

• ISO-27001 Advisory

Our experts guide your organization through the process of achieving and maintaining compliance with the ISO-27001 standard for information security management systems.

• IT Risk Assessment

We conduct detailed IT risk assessments to identify potential vulnerabilities and provide actionable strategies to mitigate risks.

• Cybersecurity Execution Support

We offer hands-on support to implement cybersecurity strategies and initiatives, ensuring seamless execution and integration into your business processes.

• Security Strategy and Transformation

We help you develop and execute a comprehensive security strategy that evolves with your business, ensuring resilience against emerging threats.

• NIST Advisory

Our team provides advisory services based on the NIST cybersecurity framework, helping you implement best practices for managing cybersecurity risks.

With our Consulting and Advisory Services, Cyber Square Limited ensures that your organization’s security strategies are robust, future-proof, and aligned with your operational goals, enabling you to confidently navigate the complex world of cybersecurity and compliance.
