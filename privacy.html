How We Collect and Use Your Personal Information

Cyber Square Limited collects personal information to provide services to customers, enhance their experience, and comply with legal requirements. This includes:

• Basic Information: Name, job title, employer, work address, phone number, and email.

• Purpose of Collection: To deliver our services, communicate with you, and improve our offerings.

We do not sell personal information. We share it only with third-party service providers who facilitate the delivery of our services.

Use of the Cyber Square Website

Our website automatically collects certain information, including:

We use this information to analyze trends, administer the site, improve usability, and customize content.


When and How We Share Information

• Third-Party Service Providers:Personal data may be stored and processed by third-party vendors strictly for service facilitation (e.g., cloud storage, delivery of purchased items).

Cyber Square Limited ensures all third-party service providers adhere to relevant data protection laws.


International Data Transfers

Cyber Square Limited may transfer personal data to international locations where necessary. By using our services, you consent to this transfer. We implement safeguards such as binding agreements and standard data protection clauses to ensure the safety of your information.

Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR, you have the following rights:

• Access: Request access to your personal data.

• Rectification: Correct inaccuracies in your data.

• Erasure: Request deletion of your data.

• Restrict Processing: Limit how your data is processed.

• Object: Object to the processing of your data.

• Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a usable format.

• Withdraw Consent: Revoke your consent for data processing at any time.

For assistance in exercising these rights, contact info@cybersquareng.ng.

Data Storage and Retention

Your personal data is stored securely on Cyber Square Limited’s servers or on third-party cloud services. Data is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or to comply with legal and operational requirements.

Policy Updates

Cyber Square Limited may update this Privacy Policy as necessary. We will notify users of significant changes via the website or direct communication.

Cyber Square Limited is dedicated to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with applicable data protection laws.
